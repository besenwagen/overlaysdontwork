Using color as the only visual means of conveying information

Stock Ticker Name Amount of Change
FC Foo Co. 1.2
AC Acme 5.2

Explain

The table above uses color to indicate whether the stock value has fallen or risen using a red background color and green background color respectively. This is bad because there's no other mechanism of providing that information. This can be repaired by using a '-' or '+' sign. An overlay product cannot automatically fix this because it will have no idea what the colors mean and therefore no idea what to do to fix it.
