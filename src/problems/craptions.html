Explanation

This video provides captions of very low quality. As a consequence, users who are deaf or hard-of-hearing will be unable to accurately understand the content. It is impossible for an overlay product to fix this because that would require the overlay product to have the ability to access the audio content, recognize the words, and generate its own captions - something Google itself cannot sufficiently do in most cases.

Of course, as a bonus, this video also does not have audio description. That's another thing an overlay can't fix!
